Kaelan Burkett is a photographer and videographer specializing in documentation of art and architecture.

 

info@kaelanburkett.com

 

I have created work with The Metropolitan Museum of Art, Massachusetts Museum of Contemporary Art, Williams College Museum of Art, Sundance Institute, Usdan Gallery, Hampden Gallery, and Installation Space. My work has been published in newspapers, magazines, and exhibition catalogues – see below for a list of recent features.
